Animation Library: Framer Motion

framer-motion is used in 6 files and adds ~140KB to your bundle. Here's exactly what it does on your site — and your options.

What framer-motion currently powers

🃏
GlassCard

Fade-up entrance + subtle hover scale on cards in OurApproach, HowWeTeach, ProgramsWeOffer

📜
ParallaxSection

Scroll-driven vertical offset on cards — elements shift up/down as you scroll past them

🎈
FloatingElement

Infinite slow float animation (not visible anywhere on the live pages — unused at runtime)

Your options

A

Keep it — split into a separate lazy chunk

Framer-motion gets its own async bundle. It only loads after the main page renders, so it never blocks initial paint. The animations (parallax, glass cards) stay exactly as they are. Best for polish.

Bundle impact: removed from critical path. Loads ~1s after first paint.
B

Replace with CSS animations

Use the existing AnimateOnScroll component (already in your code) for card entrances. Remove parallax. Remove framer-motion entirely — saves 140KB. Best for maximum speed.

Trade-off: parallax scrolling effect and floating animation are gone.
C

Keep parallax only — replace simple entrances

Swap GlassCard entrance animations to CSS (via AnimateOnScroll), keep ParallaxSection in framer-motion. Reduces how much framer-motion does, and moves it to lazy chunk.

Middle ground: still has parallax scroll effect, less animation overhead.
